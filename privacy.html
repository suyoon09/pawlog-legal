2. How We Use Your Data

  • Core Functionality: Your pet data and health logs are used exclusively to provide the App's features — health tracking, SOAP report generation, AI observation extraction, and medication reminders.
  • On-Device AI: The App uses Apple's NaturalLanguage framework to extract clinical observations from your notes. This processing happens entirely on your device. No data is sent to any external AI service or server.
  • Authentication: Sign in with Apple credentials are stored securely in your device's Keychain and used solely to authenticate your identity within the App.
  • Push Notifications: If enabled, we use Apple's local notification system to send medication reminders. These are scheduled locally on your device — no server is involved.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or transmit your personal data or pet health data to any third party.

  • No data is sent to external servers
  • No analytics SDKs or tracking libraries are used
  • No advertising networks receive your data
  • Subscription transactions are handled entirely by Apple via StoreKit
  • SOAP reports (PDFs) are generated locally and shared only when you explicitly choose to export them

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. Authentication credentials are stored in the iOS Keychain, Apple's encrypted storage system. We do not operate any backend servers or cloud databases.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Your data persists on your device as long as the App is installed
  • You can delete individual pets, logs, or medications at any time by swiping to delete
  • You can delete your account and all associated authentication data via Settings → Delete Account & Data
  • Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data permanently

6. Children's Privacy

FurScript is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at info@klarit.io.

7. Your Rights

Under applicable privacy laws (including GDPR and Korea's PIPA 개인정보보호법), you have the right to:

  • Access all data the App stores about you (it's all on your device)

  • Revoke consent for notifications (via iOS Settings)
